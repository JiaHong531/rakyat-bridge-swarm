[POLICY_DOCUMENT_REF: JKM/BWE/2026-V2]
Pursuant to the Social Welfare Act, Section 4(a), financial disbursement of RM500 per mensem (Bantuan Warga Emas) is strictly allocated to citizens aged 60 chronological years and above. Applicants must exhibit severe mobility impairment or be completely bedridden, subject to rigorous medical verification by a certified practitioner from a government medical facility. Furthermore, the applicant's household cumulative income must not exceed the current Pendapatan Garis Kemiskinan (PGK) Purata Miskin Isi Rumah Semasa. Disbursement will be halted immediately upon the applicant's admittance to any state-funded care facility (RSK/RSMT).

[POLICY_DOCUMENT_REF: MOH/PEKA/2026-V1]
Under the Skim Peduli Kesihatan untuk Kumpulan B40 (PeKa B40), individuals aged 40 and above who are registered recipients of Sumbangan Tunai Rahmah (STR) are automatically eligible for complimentary comprehensive health screenings. This encompasses blood profiling, diabetes screening, and cholesterol diagnostics. Should subsequent intervention be required, beneficiaries may apply for a maximum health aid equipment subsidy of RM20,000, which includes intraocular lenses, hearing aids, and mobility assistance devices such as motorized wheelchairs, strictly claimable via participating Klinik Kesihatan (KK).
